Dan Friedman

I'm a second-year PhD student in the Princeton NLP group, working with Danqi Chen. Some of my current interests include unsupervised structure learning, probabilistic models, and inductive bias. I'm also interested in applications of NLP to humanities research and am involved with the Princeton Center for Digital Humanities . Before this I was a software engineer at Google and IBM, and before that I was an undergraduate at Yale where I worked with Bob Frank and Dragomir Radev, and received a BA in English.

I am on GitHub, Twitter, and email.
